## Problem
Rural people do not have easy access to doctors and hospitals. They travel long distances for small health issues.

## Solution
GramCare connects villagers with doctors through mobile and village health centers using online consultation.

## Users
- Rural patients
- Health workers
- Doctors

## Features
- Online consultation
- Digital health records
- Appointment booking
- Prescription management
